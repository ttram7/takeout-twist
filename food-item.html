Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef, browned, drained, seasoned with taco seasoning
  • 8 taco bowl shells
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 8 tbsp taco sauce
  • shredded colby cheese
  • toppings: tomatoes, sour cream, scallions

Instructions

  1. Brown drain and season the ground beef with your favorite taco seasoning.
  2. Bake the shells flat in the oven till just golden brown.
  3. Spread 4 of the shells with refried beans.
  4. Split the ground beef between the 4 shells.
  5. Spread 2 T of taco sauce on each of the remaining 4 shells.
  6. Top with cheese.
  7. Lightly broil the cheese and sauced shells till melty.
  8. Place those on top of the beef and bean shells.
  9. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Mexican Pizza

servings: 4

time: 30 min
